Cloudiness vs. Latitude

The third factor we looked at was the variation in cloudiness based on how far each city in the dataset was from the equator.

There are not any clear trends by latitude for cloudiness, though there is certainly some clustering around specific percentages (0%, 20%, 40%, 75%, 90% and 100%). We believe that this most likely has something to do with how cloud cover is reported or with specific meteorological phenomenon.
