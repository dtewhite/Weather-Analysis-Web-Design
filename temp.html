Temperature vs. Latitude

The first factor we looked at was the variation in temperature based on how far each city examined was from the equator. This seems like a silly analysis at first, but after looking at the associated image it is apparent that there is more than meets the eye.

There is a clear trend that the further from the equator a city is, the lower the maximum temperature will be. This is not unexpected. Interestingly though,there are more cities further north than there are further south, which we think is because there is actually more land mass in the northern hemisphere than in the southern (Canada, Greenland and Northern Eurasia).
